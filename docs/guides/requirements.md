# Product Requirements (MVP)

## Goal
Deliver SOC 2 audit‑ready compliance without consultants by automating scan, policy creation, remediation, and evidence collection.

## Target Users
- SaaS founders and security leads
- Compliance managers at growth‑stage companies

## Success Criteria
1. Audit‑ready in 30 days or less.
2. 80%+ of controls have automated evidence.
3. Full export in under 10 minutes.

## Core Workflows
1. Onboard → Connect integrations → Initial scan.
2. Review findings → Generate policies → Approve.
3. Remediate gaps → Evidence automatically collected.
4. Export audit package.

## Non‑Goals (MVP)
- Full multi‑framework harmonization.
- Deep GRC customization beyond templates.
- Custom auditor portal.
